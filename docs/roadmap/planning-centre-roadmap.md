## Current decision

No Planning Centre or interactive planning tool is approved for development or public release.

The former Fridge Fit and Delivery Planner was parked before implementation. Its proposed workflow required readers to collect and assess too much technical and delivery information for one purchase decision. No prototype or public route exists. The obsolete specification, test plan and delivery plan were removed during the Phase 1 documentation consolidation.

## Conditions for reconsideration

A future Planning Centre proposal requires:

1. evidence of a recurring reader problem that static guidance does not solve well;
2. a simpler interaction than the parked fridge concept;
3. a clear maintenance owner;
4. inputs and results that can be explained without false certainty;
5. compliance with the Planning Tool Standard and Tool Disclaimer Standard;
6. an accessible, privacy-minimised implementation; and
7. a fresh Editorial Decision Framework record.

## Current priority

Maintain and improve the existing publication, complete the approved imagery repairs and establish a controlled commercial workflow. Do not resume tool implementation from retired documents.

## Next review trigger

Review this decision only when search, visitor feedback or repeated reader questions show a specific planning problem that would materially benefit from an interactive tool.
