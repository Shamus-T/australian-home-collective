# Australian Home Collective publishing foundation

This directory is the version-controlled source of truth for Australian Home Collective's internal publishing standards, public editorial commitments, practical knowledge and future planning resources.

## Structure

- `governance/` — the charter, master manual and decision framework
- `editorial/` — writing standards and approved public-policy drafts
- `knowledge/` — structured practical knowledge used in guides and tools
- `legal/` — legal and risk documents as they are prepared and professionally reviewed
- `tools/` — planning-tool standards and functional specifications
- `roadmap/` — publication and Planning Centre roadmaps

Public-facing documents record their publication status and live route in their document-control table. Internal documents are operating standards and should be reviewed when the publication's scope, commercial model or risk profile changes.

## Document control

- Owner: Australian Home Collective
- Initial foundation version: 1.0
- Foundation date: 18 July 2026
- Review approach: substantive reviews only; dates are not advanced automatically
