# Phase 1 editorial triage

Updated 10 July 2026 after the category-copy humanisation pass.

## Decision key

- **Keep** — the guide earns its route and needs no structural change in Phase 1.
- **Re-scope** — keep the route, but sharpen its audience, problem or promise in a later copy pass.
- **Merge candidate** — overlap is real; consolidate only after an explicit redirect and internal-link plan.
- **Source** — keep the route, but retain or add authoritative safety/source links before expanding claims.
- **Expand later** — the category needs a missing subject before adding more similar storage pages.

No guide is being deleted in this phase. Merge candidates remain live until their replacement, redirects and links are reviewed together.
