# Kitchen Product Category Plan

## Purpose

Prepare future product-category additions for the Kitchen cluster without adding shopping links yet.

## Principles

- Do not add product links until affiliate/commercial setup is ready.
- Do not claim products have been tested unless they genuinely have been.
- Do not use current prices unless they are being pulled from a current, maintainable source.
- Prefer product-category guidance first, product recommendations later.
- Use buying checks, fit, measurements, cleaning, renter suitability and use-case matching.
- Trigger affiliate disclosure only when actual affiliate links are added.
